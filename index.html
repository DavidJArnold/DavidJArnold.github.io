I am a mathematician interested in harnessing data to provide impact to stakeholders.

 

I am a Data Scientist, currently working at QBE on Claims Analytics, developing smart tools to help claims officers.

Previously I was a mathematician, most recently as a Lecturer in Applied Mathematics in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at Macquarie University, and before that as an Assistant Adjunct Professor at the Department of Mathematics, University of California, Los Angeles. I received my PhD in Applied Mathematics from the School of Mathematical Sciences, The University of Adelaide in 2016.
